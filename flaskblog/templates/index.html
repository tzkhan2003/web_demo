EcoKnowMe

An Online Platform to Save the Environment & Modernize Urban Agriculture

In a world where environmental pollution is everyone’s prime concern, our startup idea EcoKnowMe provides a simple yet effective solution by providing 3 things

  • Map-based analytical services for awareness and suggestions
  • Community services for agrolearning, solutions and assessments
  • Numerous scopes of Urban agriculture.

We don’t want to protect the environment, we want to create a world where the environment doesn’t need protecting.

Why Choose EcoKnowMe for you?

Conscious People always want to save the environment by any means, but they lack a system that can provide optimum services to meet all of their multifarious demands. So we are introducing such a system that meets the demands of all -a Prospective Buyer, a Donator,a Volunteer, a horticulturist, an aspiring gardener or even a conscious citizen.
